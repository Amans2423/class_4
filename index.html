1️⃣ Why is a Computer Called a Machine?

💡 A machine helps us do work easily. Just like:

✅ Simple Explanation

  • 🌀 A fan cools the room
  • 🧺 Washing machine washes clothes
  • 🖥️ Computer helps us write, draw, play!

➡️ So, a computer is also a machine.

🔍 Deeper Explanation

  • 💻 Computer works with electricity
  • 🧠 It follows our instructions
  • 🔄 Works automatically, doesn’t get tired

🎥 Video Recording Steps

  1. Say: “Hello! My name is [Your Name]. Today I will tell you why a computer is a machine.”
  2. Use real machine examples: fan, washing machine, etc.
  3. Show your computer and explain
  4. Finish: “Thank you for watching!”

3️⃣ IPO Cycle (Input – Process – Output)

✅ Simple Example (Drawing)

  • ✍️ Input: Crayons and paper
  • 🖌️ Process: Drawing
  • 🖼️ Output: Beautiful picture

🔍 Deeper Example (Game)

  • 🎮 Input: Pressing keys
  • 🧠 Process: Computer checks & moves player
  • 📺 Output: Player moves on screen

🎥 Video Recording Steps

  1. Say: “Hello! Today I will show you the IPO cycle using drawing.”
  2. Show crayons and say: “Input: These are my crayons and paper.”
  3. Draw and say: “Process: I am drawing.”
  4. Show result: “Output: This is my drawing!”
  5. Smile and say: “Thank you for watching!”
